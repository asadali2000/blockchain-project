Generate Digital Identity

Create a cryptographic identity (public/private key) representing a custody role such as: Police Officer, Forensic Analyst, or Legal Authority.


⚠ IMPORTANT – Custody Digital Identity
  • Private Key must not be shared — it is used to digitally sign custody transfers.
  • Keep both keys securely stored. There is no recovery method if lost.
  • Each generated key pair represents an individual responsible for handling evidence.
